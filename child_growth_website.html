What is Stunting?

Stunting is impaired growth and development in children due to poor nutrition, repeated infections, and inadequate psychosocial stimulation.

Nutrition & Care Tips

  • Exclusive breastfeeding for the first 6 months
  • Balanced diet with essential nutrients
  • Regular health checkups and vaccinations
  • Parental education on child care
