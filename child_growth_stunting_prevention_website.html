What is Stunting?

Stunting is impaired growth and development that children experience due to poor nutrition, repeated infection, and inadequate psychosocial stimulation.

Causes of Stunting

  • Poor maternal nutrition during pregnancy
  • Inadequate breastfeeding and complementary feeding
  • Frequent infections and illnesses
  • Poor sanitation and hygiene

Prevention Tips

  • Exclusive breastfeeding for the first 6 months
  • Balanced and diverse diet after 6 months
  • Routine health checkups and vaccinations
  • Proper hygiene practices (handwashing, clean water)
